# New Employee Onboarding Guide

## First Day
1. Collect your badge from the front desk
2. Meet your assigned buddy who will guide you through the first week
3. Complete mandatory security training (takes ~1 hour)
4. Set up your laptop with IT support (Room 204)
5. Attend the welcome lunch at 12:30 PM

## First Week
- Complete all compliance training modules in the learning portal
- Set up your development environment (see Tech FAQ)
- Schedule 1:1 meetings with your team members
- Review the team's current sprint board in Jira
- Join relevant Slack channels: #general, #team-<your-team>, #random

## First Month Goals
- Complete your first code contribution (bug fix or small feature)
- Attend at least one design review meeting
- Complete the "Architecture Overview" self-paced course
- Have a 30-day check-in with your manager

## Key Contacts
- IT Support: it-help@company.com or Slack #it-support
- HR Questions: hr@company.com
- Facilities: facilities@company.com
- Security Team: security@company.com

## Benefits Enrollment
You have 30 days from your start date to enroll in benefits. Access the benefits portal at benefits.company.com. Options include health, dental, vision insurance, 401k matching up to 6%, and flexible spending accounts.
