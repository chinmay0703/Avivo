# Company Policies

## Work Hours
Standard work hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Flexible work arrangements are available upon manager approval. Core hours (when all employees should be available) are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Full remote arrangements require VP-level approval. All remote workers must have reliable internet (minimum 50 Mbps) and a dedicated workspace.

## Leave Policy
- Annual Leave: 20 days per year (accrued monthly)
- Sick Leave: 12 days per year
- Personal Days: 3 days per year
- Parental Leave: 16 weeks paid for primary caregiver, 4 weeks for secondary caregiver

## Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to treat colleagues with respect and professionalism. Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited. Report concerns to HR or use the anonymous reporting hotline.

## Equipment
New employees receive a company laptop and monitor. Additional equipment requests go through the IT portal. Equipment must be returned upon separation from the company.
